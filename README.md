## 🌟 About The Project

**CivicBridge** is a revolutionary community problem-solving platform that connects citizens, experts, and local authorities to collaboratively identify, discuss, and resolve community issues. By leveraging crowdsourced intelligence and AI-powered scoring, we transform how communities tackle local challenges.

### 🎯 Problem Statement
Traditional community problem reporting systems are:
- ❌ Slow and bureaucratic
- ❌ Lack transparency
- ❌ Don't leverage community wisdom
- ❌ Poor tracking and follow-up

### 💡 Our Solution
CivicBridge provides:
- ✅ **Fast problem reporting** with images and location
- ✅ **Community-driven solutions** from diverse perspectives
- ✅ **AI-powered solution ranking** based on impact and feasibility
- ✅ **Transparent tracking** from report to resolution
- ✅ **Gamification** to encourage active participation

## ✨ Key Features

### 👥 For Citizens
- 🔍 **Browse & Search** problems by category, location, severity
- 📝 **Report Problems** with photos, descriptions, and location
- 💡 **Propose Solutions** with detailed implementation plans
- 🗳️ **Vote & Comment** on problems and solutions
- 🏆 **Earn Credibility** through quality contributions
- 📊 **Track Impact** of your contributions

### 👨‍💼 For Experts
- 🎯 **Submit Expert Solutions** with technical details
- 📈 **Get Recognition** through solution adoption
- 🤝 **Collaborate** with other experts
- 📚 **Build Portfolio** of solved community issues

### 👑 For Admins
- 📊 **Analytics Dashboard** with real-time metrics
- ✅ **Moderate Content** (problems, solutions, comments)
- 🎯 **Adopt Solutions** and mark problems resolved
- 📁 **Export Data** for reporting
- 👥 **User Management** and role assignment

### 🏛️ For Government/Authorities
- 📋 **Official Verification** of problems
- 💰 **Budget Tracking** for solutions
- 📈 **Performance Metrics** for departments
- 🔗 **API Integration** with existing systems

### 🤖 AI-Powered Features
- 🧮 **Smart Solution Scoring** (Impact 40%, Feasibility 30%, Cost 20%, Credibility 10%)
- 🔄 **Duplicate Detection** for similar problems
- 🏷️ **Auto-categorization** of reported issues
- 📊 **Predictive Analytics** for problem trends
